Join Us for Our November Meetup

{{ page.description }}

PHP and WordPress programmers: Help Big Heavy World represent Vermont's bands and musicians in a new Vermont band guide at bigheavyworld.com! The site is powered by WordPress and the volunteer crew has been gathering contact info for musicians across Vermont. Volunteers will build a directory that shares Vermont's amazing music scene with the world, and connect it to the New England-wide CreativeGround.org directory so Vermont can shine.
