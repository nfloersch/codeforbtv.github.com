L3D Cube Assembly Party

At the upcoming National Day of Civic Hacking, participants will be creating prototypes for the large 3D matrix of 52,000 individually-programmable LEDs on the glass front of the ECHO Lake Aquarium and Science Center building. One of the prototyping tools that will be available are about a dozen L3D Cubes; however, we need to assemble them first. Come by and help put one together!

Collaborate on Community and Civic Projects

We welcome everyone to join us and work together to improve their communities.

Code for BTV Hack Nights are a great opportunity to meet and collaborate with others working on community and civic projects. Whether you are a coder, designer, project manager, or are curious about how you can get involved, please join us. We need a wide variety of skills, experience and perspectives.
