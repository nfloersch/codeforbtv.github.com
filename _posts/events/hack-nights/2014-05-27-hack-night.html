Join Us for an Event Preview of National Day of Civic Hacking

Not sure what to expect at National Day of Civic Hacking? Join Code for BTV and Girl Develop It Burlington for an event preview, where we'll explore some of the projects for National Day of Civic Hacking.

Participants can also continue to work on the civic hacking projects that are actively being developed and/or maintained.
