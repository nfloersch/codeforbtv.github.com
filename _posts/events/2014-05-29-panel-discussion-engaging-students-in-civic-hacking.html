Presentation & Panel Discussion

Join Burlington Mayor Miro Weinberger, Code for America Brigade Captains Bradley Holt and Jason Pelletier, and representatives from Burlington-area higher education institutions for a presentation and panel discussion on how to engage students in civic hacking through Code for BTV. Civic hacking is a participatory effort to improve communities through technology and is an opportunity for students to gain experience by working on real-world projects.
