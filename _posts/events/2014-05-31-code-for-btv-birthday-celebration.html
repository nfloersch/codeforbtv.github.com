Code for BTV Turns One!

Celebrate the first year of Code for BTV on the Burlington waterfront starting after the first day of National Day of Civic Hacking. Families and friends are welcome!

This birthday celebration will be part of other festivities happening on the Burlington waterfront that evening. Pathways Past/Present, located in close proximity at the Moran Plant, combines interactive installations and performances by local high school students and professional artists. Food will also be available for purchase.
