City of Burlington

{{ page.description }}

The City of Burlington's Mayor's Office is a municipal partner of Code for BTV. We have collaborated on numerous events and the City of Burlington's open data platform. We have also collaborated with numerous departments and programs of the City of Burlington, and look forward to continuing this work together.

Read the Letter of Support from the Mayor's Office.

Municipal Collaborations

Code for BTV has collaborated with these departments and programs of the City of Burlington:

  • Mayor's Office
  • Fletcher Free Library
  • CEDO
  • Burlington Telecom
  • Burlington Parks, Recreation & Waterfront
  • Burlington Electric Department
  • Burlington City Arts
  • BTV Ignite
